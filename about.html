About Laserdiscourse

Welcome!

My name is Frank Burley from Portland, OR….. yeah, I know. I started this site as a side project for fun as well as something that might help others interested in starting up their laserdisc hobby. People that want to take a tour as long time hobbyists may find a little gem in my posts. I do not claim to be an expert in all things laserdiscs and I don’t think I ever will be. Like many others, it’s just for the fun of it. I realize that people will disagree with some of the info I have provided.

Enjoy!
