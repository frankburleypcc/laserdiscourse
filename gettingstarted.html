Getting Started

Everyone has a different path from finding a seller on facebook to ordering one off of eBay to Craigslist. These players are not going to be found at Best Buy so you’ll need to do some hunting around. That’s part of fun!

For me, my first player came from a garage sale on a Sunday morning. I picked up the MODEL NUMBER for $15 and it even came with the remote. I always keep an eye on Craigslist because occasionally people are ready to give up their collection for a small price, or they are cleaning out Dad’s basement and don’t even know about these gems.

You’ll need to find a way to hook your LD player to your TV. Your best bet is to use the yellow composite cable for the video and red and white for the audio. It's been said that you will not see an improvement with S-Video but feel free to try. You will not find HDMI on these!

Find a few movies that you would like to try out and sit back and have fun.
